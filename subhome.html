RIGHT TO PLAY:

Right To Play(RTP) is an award winning social enterprice dedicated to using sport for the holistic development of youth, through direct implementation of sport programs, training of sports trainers and supporting grassroots sports administration. Right To Play(RTP) is a social enterprise set-up in 2018 by our team to work towards the vision of developing sport and empowering youth at the grassroots in India.
