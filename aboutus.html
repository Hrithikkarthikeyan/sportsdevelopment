ABOUT US

1. RTP is initially started with the aim of nurturing young sporting talent, especially amongst marginalized sections of our society.

2. RTP's mantra that sport is a powerful tool in supporting the holistic development of youth and that providing them with well structured and professionally conducted programs fosters their growth as confident and competent learners.

3. RTP aims to promote a sporting culture among the youth that is inclusive, sustainable and engaging,. especially for marginalized sections of society who find it harder to gain access to sport.

4. RTP has been supporting the development of youth and sport in India at the grassroots since 2018, working across 12 states in India including Delhi, Odisha, Andhra pradesh, Telangana, Haryana, Uttar pradesh, Gujurat, Madhya pradesh, Chattisgarh, West bengal, Bihar, and Rajasthan.
